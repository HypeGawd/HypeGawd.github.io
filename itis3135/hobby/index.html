This website is about all of the cars that I have owned so far!

I have always been a big car guy. Over then past few years I have bought and sold many cars.













Subaru Outback

This was my 1st car, a 2008 Subaru Outback.

Fun fact - I have driven this Car over 30,000 miles since I have had it.

I used this car to deliver pizza for over a year and have gone on many road trips with this car.

I still own this car today and use it to drive back and forth from Charlotte NC to Morrisville NC.













Toyota Sienna

This was my 2nd car, a 2000 Toyota Sienna.

Fun fact - This car had over 376,000 miles when I sold it.

I used this vehicle as a cargo/work van for a couple months before I sold it to a friend.













Toyota Camry

This was my 3rd car, a 2000 Toyota Camry.

Fun fact - The dent on the hood was from the previous owner hitting a person on a bicycle.

I kept this car as my "backup car" for a couple months before selling it for a $800 profit.









Honda Civic

This was my 4th car, a 2005 Honda Civic.

Fun fact - I bought this car to learn how to drive a manual transmission.

I still own this car today and use it when I want to just take a drive on the weekends.
